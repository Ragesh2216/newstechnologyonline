FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common things people ask us.

How do I submit a story tip or press release? +

Email our editorial team at tips@nextech.news with a brief summary and any supporting links or documents. We review all submissions within 48 hours and our editors will reach out if we decide to pursue the story.

How can I access the NexTech News API? +

Head to our Services page to learn about our API plans. You can sign up for a free developer tier to get started. For enterprise licensing, contact api@nextech.news with your use case.

Do you accept sponsored content or advertorials? +

We maintain strict editorial independence. We do not publish advertorials or sponsored content within our news feed. For brand partnerships and display advertising, contact partners@nextech.news for our media kit.

How can I report an error in an article? +

We take accuracy seriously. Email hello@nextech.news with the article URL and a description of the error. Our editorial team will investigate and issue a correction within 24 hours if warranted.

Are there open positions at NexTech News? +

Yes! We're growing fast. While our formal careers page is coming soon, you can email hello@nextech.news with your CV and the role you're interested in — we review all applications.
