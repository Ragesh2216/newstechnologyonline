Our Journey

Milestones & History

From a two-person newsletter to the world's leading tech news platform.

2019
🚀 Founded in San Francisco

Aisha and Marcus launch NexTech as a weekly newsletter from a garage with just 500 subscribers.

2020
📈 100K Readers Milestone

Pivoted to a full web platform during the pandemic. Reached 100,000 monthly readers in 8 months and closed $1.2M in seed funding.

2021
🤖 AI Curation Engine Launched

Dr. Lena Vasquez joins. NexTech launches its proprietary AI-powered news curation engine.

2022
💰 Series A: $18M Raised

Raised $18M from Sequoia and a16z. Expanded to 45 journalists across 12 countries.

2023
📡 News API Platform

Launched NexTech Developer Platform. 500+ companies now power their products with our real-time News API.

2024
🌍 1 Million Readers

Crossed 1M monthly active readers. Won "Best Tech Publication" at Digital Media Awards for the second year running.

2026
🎯 1.2M+ & Growing

With 1.2M+ monthly readers, offices in SF, London, and Singapore, and 12,500+ articles published — the mission continues.
